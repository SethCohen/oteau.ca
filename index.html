About Us

In the Fall of 2019, Tea Club was founded by a group of five dedicated tea lovers. The goal of this club was to bring everyone together to drink tea, meet new people, provide a safe space, and have fun! Since Fall of 2019, the OTeaU has grown to be a community of over a couple hundred students.

OTeaU hosts a variety of events that include gaming and movie nights, general socializing, and study spaces (that cater for both loud and quiet studying). Of course, all of them catered with tea. While in person, OTeaU allows for tea exchanges and tea testing of a wide selection.

Come join us for tea tastings, giveaways, exchanges, conversation, or just relax and de-stress from uni life with a hot cup of tea!

How To Join

If you'd like to formally join the club, feel free to fill out this form.

We also highly recommend joining us on Discord, as that is our main form of communication between club members and the first place we post to for club news and events.
You can also follow us on our socials on Instagram and Twitter.

For any additional questions, feel free to email us at ontarioteau@gmail.com
